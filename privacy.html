Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 6, 2026

Introduction

Cloud Motion Lab LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Magnifying Glass Pro mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Magnifying Glass Pro is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Camera Access: The app requires camera access to function as a magnifier. Camera images are processed locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Photos (Save Only): If you choose to save a frozen image, the app writes to your photo library. We do not read or access your existing photos.
  • Purchase Information: Subscription and purchase data is handled entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not collect or store payment information.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Unity Ads: We display ads to free-tier users. Unity Technologies may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad delivery and measurement. You can review Unity's privacy policy at unity.com/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Apple StoreKit: In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple. Apple's privacy policy applies to all transactions.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Location data
  • Camera images or video
  • Contacts, messages, or call logs
  • Health or biometric data

Data Storage

All app settings (zoom level, preferences) are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage mechanisms. No data is transmitted to external servers by our app.

Children's Privacy

Magnifying Glass Pro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
